What guides us

A practitioner's standard

Exceptional Provenance

Every cigar in the humidor is selected by Amir Nadimi personally. Three decades spent tracking soil, climate, and craft mean you will never find a filler here. Only the genuine article, stored and presented exactly as it should be.

Unmatched Expertise

Amir speaks the language of draw, construction, and flavour evolution with the precision of a craftsman. Whether you are exploring your first cigar or chasing a rare vitola, the knowledge on offer here is real and freely shared.

True Accessibility

A private club with an open door. Amir has spent his career moving against the grain of gatekeeping. Seasoned aficionado or curious newcomer, you will find the same genuine welcome, the same honest guidance, and the same quality in the hand.

The founder

There is a fundamental difference between a purveyor and an authority. To be a purveyor is to manage inventory. To be an authority is to curate an experience, preserve a culture, and pass down the nuanced art of the leaf.

Amir Nadimi founded CNX Cigars on thirty years with the leaf, and on a single, clear idea: that the best cigar is the one shared. He built this lounge as a community, not a shop. A confluence point for expatriates, travellers, and local connoisseurs who understand that slowing down to smoke a fine cigar is an act of mindfulness.
